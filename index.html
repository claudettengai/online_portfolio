About Me

Hi, I’m Claudette. I’m a Bay Area native and recent UC Berkeley graduate with a degree in Cognitive Science. I like brains. I like language. And I like people. I know how to listen to people and hear what they’re actually saying. For example, did you pick up that in general I prefer interdisciplinary fields (like cog sci or ux), that there’s a pretty large possibility that Keak da Sneak was at some point my top-played artist or maybe just that I'm kind of a nerd?
I cannot know what I don’t know, but I do know that it's a lot, and that I want to learn it. A crazy number of brain-imaging studies have suggested that comprehension and retention are positively correlated with level of emotional response. Basically, you understand and remember the things that you care about. Well, I care about my users and I believe can make my users care about my product too.
