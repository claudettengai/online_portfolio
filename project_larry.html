Larry Life Coaching

Business Website

Summary

I designed a website for my client, Larry’s life coaching business. We wanted to keep the website simple, making it as easy as possible to contact the office and sign up as a new customer; it is also designed for users who don’t know what life coaching is, or whether it’s the right step for them, but are curious to find out more. We used large, powerful pictures of paths in nature to highlight the theme of embarking on a journey and starting a new phase in life.
