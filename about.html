Hi, I'm Claudette.

I’m a Bay Area native and recent UC Berkeley graduate with a degree in Cognitive Science. I like brains. I like language. And I like people. JK Rowling blows me away because as epic as warring good vs evil in a fantasy dimension of killer magics and questionable fashion undoubtedly is, it’s her infinitely complex and endlessly multifaceted characters that really facilitate the story. My experience still changes with each read (there’ve been a lot), a phenomenon that I like to ascribe to my continually growing arsenal of experiences and therefore my continually expanding potential to empathize with the different personalities in the story. Empathy motivates me to learn. Empathy broadens my ability to comprehend. Empathy inspires my designs. I know how to listen to people and hear what they’re actually saying. For example, did you pick up that I like networking models of cognition and that in general I prefer interdisciplinary fields (like cog sci or ux)? That I’ll often forgo correct grammar for the sake of impact, or that there’s a pretty large possibility that Keak da Sneak was at some point my top-played artist? Or maybe just that I’m kind of a nerd?

I cannot know what I don’t know, but I do know that it’s a hell of a lot, and that I want to learn it. Way too many brain-imaging studies have suggested that comprehension and retention are positively level of emotional response. Basically, you understand and remember the things that you care about. I care about my users, and I can make my users care about my product also.
