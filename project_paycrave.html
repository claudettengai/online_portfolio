Paycrave

iOS mobile app

Summary

Paycrave is a mobile app designed to allow food truck fans to locate, discover, rate, review and purchase from nearby food trucks.

Checking out the Competition

The first step was to find out what already exists and is successful in the foodspace. I looked at direct competition (other food truck apps) as well as other food-related apps that might have features that I wanted to include in Paycrave.
